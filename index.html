The Author

The author grew up in a small town in the Okanagan Valley of British Columbia where both her parents instilled into her and her siblings a love of books. She has always enjoyed being a storyteller, but did not begin to write seriously until she acquired her first computer in the 90’s.


After high school she trained as a nurse, married a widower with three young children and switched careers from nursing to wife and motherhood. She and her husband travelled extensively all through their marriage. Now a widow, with three children, four grandchildren and one great grandson, she maintains close ties with her family and writes at her leisure.

She is the author of two published books.

Come Fill Up My Cup

“Come fill Up My Cup” is a love story mostly set in Scotland. Robin Lindsay is a middle-aged Canadian widow who, on a trip to Scotland, meets a similarly aged widower quite by chance. James MacLachlan is still haunted by his wife’s death 8 years before, but for the first time finds himself attracted to another woman. The couple fall in love, but events from the past and a brutal murder in the present complicate matters.
